Courses Details


  • Our workshop is aimed at participants with diverse backgrounds with an interest to learn about constructing an aircraft which will make the participants innovate in the design stage, put their design into practice in the manufacturing stage and see their hard work take-off through the hands of our expert flyers at the end. It provides an opportunity to experience the true spirit of engineering.
  • Workshop duration: 1/2 days

Content:

  • Introduction to CAD
    • What is CAD, ED
    • 2D & 3D CAD.
    • Different CAD software available in market
    • Competitive info.

  • Working with SolidWorks
    • SolidWorks intuitive interface.
    • Different module of SolidWorks and its uses.
    • Task pane features.
    • Versions and changes in SolidWorks.

  • 2D Sketching
    • Sketching Interface
    • Default planes & adding new planes
    • Sketching entities like line, rectangle, circle, arcs etc
    • Adding relations.
  • Part Modeling
    • Modeling approaches
    • Features (Boss Extrude & Cut, Revolve, patterns, fillets etc.)
    • Instant 3D
    • Hole Wizard
    • Parent-child relationships

  • Part Drawings & detailing
    • Drawing Interface
    • Types of drawing sheets
    • Importing Models and model items

  • Simulation Xpress
    • Use of simulation
    • Analysis for stresses, deformations & FOS
    • Design optimization
